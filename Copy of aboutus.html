About Us


Our Products

Sheldon Farm is set on an idyllic 75 acres with a pond, evergreen tree orchard, peach orchard and acres of raspberries. We are located in Barre, the heart of Central Mass. Our basket business is a locally run cottage industry. The baskets are all handmade from the highest quality of reed. We offer handpainted baskets as well as baskets with dyed reed. The baskets are high quality and come in a variety of shapes and sizes. There is a style for everyone!

Susan Morello : Basket Weaver
