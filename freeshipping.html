Our Shipping Process

To make the checkout process as easy as possible, Sheldon Farm Baskets offers free shipping on all products within the Continental United States.

(*Shipping outside of the Continental United States may require an additional shipping fee. We do not ship to foreign locations at this time).


Our Service Provider : United States Postal Service

We ship all of our products via Priority Mail Flat Rate Shipping with delivery confirmation through the United States Postal Service. Our products will be processed and mailed between 5-10 business days from the time of purchase. We are a small two person operation and try to mail out our products as fast as possible. If you have an urgent request for a product, email us at including your product link. Though we cannot guarantee to expedite the process; we will try!


Package Contents

Your Package will include the following:
   - Your Purchased Product
   - Purchase Receipt
   - Sheldon Farms Business Collateral (card and note)


Issues with our Shipping Services?

Sheldon Farms Baskets wants all of our customers to walk away happy. We are willing to work with you in the event of a shipping mishap or issue. Please email us if you have any questions or concerns and we will promptly respond . For more information about refund and return policy please view our Site Policy Page.
