Our Ordering Process is as easy as 1-2-3

Placing an order through SheldonFarmBaskets.com takes 4 Steps:

   STEP 1: Selecting a Category
   STEP 2: Selecting a Product
   STEP 3: Adding a Product to Your Cart
   STEP 4: Processing Your Payment Securely Through PayPal


STEP 1: Selecting a Category

Once you are located on the Shop Online Page, the first step is to select a category. All of our products are organized by category, so that they are easily searchable. Their are currently 3 Categories that you can select from:

Category 1: All Products. This category contains all of the baskets currently available through the SheldonFarmBaskets.com website. Baskets (products) from both the "Painted Panel Baskets" and the "Unpainted Baskets" Categories can be found here.

Category 2: Painted Panel Baskets. This category contains all of the baskets which have hand painted panels on them. The hand painted panel baskets come in a wide range of sizes, shapes and styles.

Category 3: Unpainted Baskets. This category contains all of the baskets which do not contain hand painted panels, and are more traditional primitive country baskets. Like the hand painted baskets, these baskets come in a wide range of sizes, shapes and styles.

Once you have found the category you are interested in, press the "View Category" button located directly beneath the category image.


STEP 2: Selecting a Product

Once you have navigated to the category page, you can now select a product. Browse the products images and details. When you find a product you are interested in, select the "View Product" button located beneath the product image.


STEP 3: Adding a Product to Your Cart

Once you have navigated to the product detail page, you can now view more images of the product or add the product to your cart.

To view more images of the product, click on the product image. As long as you have javascript enabled, the image will enlarge on your screen.

To add the product to your cart, click on the "Add To Cart" button located beneath the product image. This button will navigate you to the corresponding PayPal cart page.


STEP 4: Processing Your Payment Through PayPal

Once you are located in the PayPal Cart, you can either continue shopping by selecting the "Continue Shopping" button or proceed to checkout by selecting the "Proceed to checkout" button.

The actual transaction will be processed securely through PayPal. PayPal accepts all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard® , American Express, and Discover (without requiring a PayPal account). If you have a PayPal account, you can process the payment through your account as well.

Why PayPal? We selected PayPal due to its ease of use for customers and wide selection of Payment Options. Oh and forgot to mention, it's the most secure as well.


ORDER COMPLETE!
